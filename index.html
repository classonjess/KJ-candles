You want it, We got it

      Beeswax:

    Helps clean the air while burning by reducing pollutants that cause asthma and allergies. Burns cleaner and longer than paraffin and soy candles.

      Paraffin Wax

    Has a high melting point to ensure the strength needed for votives and tall pillar candles. Many candles and dyes are formulated specifically for paraffin, so fragrances and colors are more potent when used with this fuel.

      Soy Wax

    Don't contain pollutants and won't produce petrol-carbon soot like other materials. he wax burns cooler therefore allowing the candles to 30-50% longer.

      Palm Wax

    It is biodegradable for eco-friendly disposal. It is created with fatty acids separated from palm oil under extreme temperatures and pressures.

      Liquid Wax

    Made with oils like mineral or paraffin oil, these burn without producing smoke and soot. These candles are great alternative to normal candles as you will not have to deal with any melted wax.

      Bayberry Wax

    A plant-based, eco-friendly wax that is combined with beeswax to create a more rigid candles.
